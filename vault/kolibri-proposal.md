## Developer Proposal: Enhancing Game Systems & AI Logic

This section details a proposal by **Marko Stanković**, a Unity & C# Developer, outlining how specialized development skills can meaningfully accelerate game teams, particularly within the context of core simulation and management mechanics.

***

### 🎯 Opportunity Identified (Targeting *Idle Miner Tycoon* Core Systems)

After analyzing the core systems of successful simulation titles (e.g., *Idle Miner Tycoon*), the primary opportunity lies in evolving passive management systems into engaging, character-driven mechanics.

**Current Limitation:** Manager systems are currently treated as mere numerical multipliers, lacking personality or observable behavior.

**Proposed Enhancement Areas:**
*   **Reactive Managers:** Implementing behavioral states that dynamically respond to in-game metrics, such as mine load, current resource levels, and prestige tier achievements.
*   **Adaptive Decision-Making:** Developing managers with apparent personality, moving beyond passive scaling to exhibit strategic, noticeable decision-making.
*   **Observable Autonomy:** Providing players with tangible visual feedback that their team is actively "working," which is critical for building player pride and increasing long-term retention.
